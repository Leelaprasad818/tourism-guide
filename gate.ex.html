Suggested Duration - 1-2 hours

What To Expect?

Ideal place to enjoy cool sea breeze, watch the city skyline light up as evening sets in, feed pigeons, click fun photos and catch a good meal at one of the nearby eateries.

Tips

  • Open throughout the day but best enjoyed at night when lit up.
  • Spend an evening at the rooftop cafes in hotels located along the coastline.
  • Take a ferry from here to Elephanta Caves or hire boats for short evening cruises.
